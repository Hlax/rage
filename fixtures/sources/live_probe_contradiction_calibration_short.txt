Controlled source for divergent-condition claim extraction in mini-run suite.

In ideation tasks under a divergent condition, AI-assisted brainstorming
increased idea diversity when participants were instructed to generate multiple
divergent directions before elaborating any single idea.

In the default condition ideation tasks, AI assistance narrowed semantic spread
across submitted ideas, consistent with prior short-form writing findings.

Limitations: diversity was rated rather than embedding-based; tasks were general
ideation prompts only.

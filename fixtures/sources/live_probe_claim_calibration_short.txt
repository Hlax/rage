In a controlled study of short-form writing tasks, we found that AI-assisted
brainstorming increased average idea quality across submitted ideas. Independent
raters scored ideas produced with AI assistance higher on usefulness and coherence
in this short-form writing task setting.

However, AI-assisted brainstorming reduced semantic diversity across submitted
ideas in the same short-form writing task setting.

Limitations: the sample consisted of undergraduate students from introductory
writing courses. Results may not generalize to film, music, or professional
creative work.
